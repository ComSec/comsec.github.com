Remarks and Future Research
•The GH-PKC is implemented in C++ and Java and Blackberry JDE.  Analysis is done with Java implementation on PC and on the Blackberry simulator. •Performance improvement of GH-DH key agreement protocol over the traditional DH-key agreement protocol can be seen clearly. •Although improvement of GH-DSA cannot be seen easily, the process time is comparable to that of DSS. •Improvements can be more easily seen in hardware implementation as the modulus length in GH-PKC is only one-third of that in DH and DSS.  Future research!
