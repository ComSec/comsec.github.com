WLAN IEEE 802.11a
  • OFDM modulation scheme
  • Frequency band of 5 GHz
  • Data rates up to 54 Mbps
  • 52  sub-carriers
    • 48 data + 5 pilot channels
  • Chip duration 50ns
  • Symbol frequency 4us
  • Sampling rate 20MHz
  • Power consumption 3.4mW
17
DVB-H
  • Support video broadcasting on
    • 2.5/3G mobile phones & handheld devices
    • MPEG-2 format
  • 5-8 MHz frequency band
  • 3409 sub-carriers
  • Up to 15 Mbps for HDTV broadcasting
  • Convolutional and Reed Solomon encoding

Conclusions
  • OFDM
    • Avoid frequency selective fading
    • Suffer high PAPR
  • Research for new sequences
    • Good cross correlation
    • Low PAPR
  • Proposed modulation scheme for 4G
    • OFDM + CDMA => MC-CDMA
