OFDM
  • Transmit data over multiple carriers
    • Narrowband sub-carriers
    • Equally-spaced orthogonal carriers
    • Parallel transmission
  • Advantage:
    • Frequency non-selective fading
    • Frequency diversity
  • Disadvantage:
    • Constructive interference among sub-carriers
    • High Peak-to-Average Power Ratio

Research in Spreading Sequences
  • Criteria for Spreading Sequences
    • Ideal 2-level autocorrelation
    • Small Cross correlations
    • Low Peak-to-average power ratio
  • P.V Kumar and O. Moreno 1991
    • Smaller cross correlations for non binary sequences
  • For GF(3n), GF(5n) and GF(7n)
    • Exhaustive search for ideal 2-level autocorrelation non-binary sequences

Conclusions
