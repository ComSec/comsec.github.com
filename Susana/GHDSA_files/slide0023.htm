GH-DSA: Signing Process
•Signing Process:
1.Randomly choose k, with 0 < k < Q and gcd(k,Q) = 1.  Use DSEA algorithm to compute (sk-1, sk, sk+1) and its dual such that gcd(sk, Q) = 1.  r = sk
2.Compute h=h(m), where h() is a hash function
3.Solve for t in the signing equation: h º xr + kt mod Q Þ t º k-1(h - rx) mod Q
•(r, t) is a digital signature of the message m.  Alice sends Bob (m, r, t) together with (sk, sk+1) and its dual.
