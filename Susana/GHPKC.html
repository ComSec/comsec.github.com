Gong-Harn Public-key Cryptosystem (GH-PKC)

With the emergence of the 3G (third-generation) networks for mobile communications, data security becomes even more important. Designing cryptosystems that meet both the power contraints and computing constraints of mobile units is very challenging. The GH-PKC reduces the size of the modulus and speeds up the computations of the same degree of security as existing cryptosystems. My research focus is on software implementation of the GH-PKC and analysis on its performance over the existing cryptosystems.
