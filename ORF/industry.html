Reverer Security

Founded in 2008 and headquartered in Dallas, Texas, United States, revere security specializes in cryptographic data security solutions for small, power-constrained chips and devices where current commercially available security technology cannot be applied. The company solves data secruity problems in embedded systems, radio frequency communications, sensor technologies, mobile computing, and applications of advanced and emerging technologies. Pitney Bowes

Founded in 1920 and headquartered in Stamford, Connecticut, United States, Pitney Bowes (PB) is a $5.6 billion company that employs 35,000 worldwide. Pitney Bowes provides mail processing equipment and integrated mail solutions for organizations worldwide. It operates in two business groups, Mailstream Solutions and Mailstream Services, and serves over 2 million businesses of all sizes through dealer and direct operations. (more information)

Research In Motion

Founded in 1984 and headquartered in Waterloo, Ontario, Canada, Research In Motion (RIM) is a leading designer, manufacture and marketer of innovative wireless solutions for the worldwide mobile communication market. RIM's portfolio of award winning products, services and embedded technologies include the BlackBerry® wireless platform, the RIM Wireless handledTM product line, software development tools, and software/hardware licensing agreements.
